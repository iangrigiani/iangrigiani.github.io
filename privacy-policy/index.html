This privacy policy applies to the game Rotrix, developed by Nacho Rasti.

Information Collection

The game uses Google Firebase and Google Play Games Services, services provided by Google LLC, to collect technical, usage, and gameplay-related information in order to improve stability, performance, and player experience.

The information collected may include, but is not limited to:

Device identifiers

Usage data and performance statistics

Crash and error reports

Google Play Games player ID

Achievement progress and unlock status

The game does not collect personally identifiable information such as physical address, phone number, or contact details.

Third-Party Services

The game uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, or related services)

Google Play Games Services (login and achievements)

These services may collect and process information in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can review Google’s privacy policy at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to:

Authenticate players using Google Play Games

Enable and display achievements

Improve the user experience

Analyze game performance

Detect and fix errors

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting user information and do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except as required for the operation of Google services.

Children’s Information

The game is not specifically directed to children but may be used by players of any age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
Users can manage or revoke Google Play Games access through their Google account settings.
